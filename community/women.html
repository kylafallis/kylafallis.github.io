Women Empowerment Initiatives

Projects and leadership roles dedicated to advocating for and supporting young women in my community.

Girl Up Club Founder & President

Role: Founder & President | Date: 2023 - 2025

I founded and led Northwest Ohio's only chapter of the United Nations' Girl Up initiative. In this role, I curated a powerful exhibit on sexual harassment, organized STEM outreach programs for young girls, and ran successful community supply drives to support local women's shelters.

Learn About Girl Up

Distinguished Young Woman of Allen County

Role: Winner & State Runner-Up | Date: February 2025

I was honored to be named the Distinguished Young Woman of Allen County, a program that recognizes excellence in scholastics, leadership, and talent. I later placed as the runner-up for the state of Ohio, serving as a role model for young women in my community.

Feminine Hygiene Supply Drives

Role: Organizer

As part of my work with the Girl Up Club, I independently organized and executed multiple feminine hygiene supply drives in local schools to address student needs, combat period poverty, and promote community welfare.
